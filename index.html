Paul Manafort, the former Trump campaign manager, was held in isolation on Rikers Island to protect him from the violence that rages on in the facility. This sort of isolation is not considered solitary confinement; Manfort was in as “private, self-contained living unit” that was larger than other units, which included a bathroom, shower, telephone, and laptop access, according to court records. Capone's stay in prison was originally pretty cushy but eventually he was sent to the infamous Alcatraz prison, where he was stabbed and got into fights and according to some reports ended up in solitary confinement. Capone eventually went insane from untreated syphilis that destroyed his brain: "He often failed to follow the guards’ orders even at the penalty of severe punishment, less out of defiance than out of an inability to intellectually process them."
